Who we are?

iSmart Lifestyle Solutions is a forward-thinking organization dedicated to rural transformation. Established in 2021 in Tripura, we work at the intersection of enterprise, agriculture, and technology—building sustainable livelihoods and empowering communities.

Our Purpose

to empower, enable, and transform

Our purpose is to create opportunities where they are needed the most.

We strive to empower individuals with the skills, knowledge, and support required to build sustainable livelihoods and independent futures.

By connecting communities to resources, markets, and technology, we aim to turn potential into progress.

VISION

Our vision is to create a future where individuals and communities are empowered through opportunities, innovation, and sustainable growth—enabling them to thrive with confidence, independence, resilience, and equal access to a better quality of life. We envision a society where innovation, entrepreneurship, and collaboration drive lasting social and economic transformation.

MISSION

  • Empower individuals through skills and entrepreneurship.
  • Enable access to finance, resources, and opportunities.
  • Connect communities to markets, technology, and financial inclusion.
  • Promote climate-resilient agriculture and sustainable growth.

The Challenges

Tripura holds immense potential in entrepreneurship, agriculture, and small businesses.

However, many MSMEs and aspiring entrepreneurs still face challenges in accessing skills, finance, and markets.

iSmart Lifestyle Solutions exists to bridge these gaps—unlocking opportunities and enabling sustainable growth.

Our Services

We design and deliver high-impact interventions across multiple sectors to build sustainable livelihoods and inclusive growth.

Building Entrepreneurs

Through structured Entrepreneurship Development Programs (EDP), we transform ideas into viable businesses.

From business planning to financial access, we guide every step.

Agriculture & Livelihoods

We promote modern and sustainable agricultural practices by integrating science, innovation, and local knowledge.

Through improved techniques, resource optimization, and value-added activities, we enhance productivity, strengthen livelihoods, and create pathways for long-term income generation.

Skill & Capacity Building

We strengthen capabilities by equipping individuals with practical skills and industry-relevant knowledge. Through focused training and capacity-building initiatives, we enable people to enhance their livelihoods and build sustainable enterprises.

  • Handloom & Handicrafts — Preserving traditional skills while improving design, quality, and market readiness
  • Bamboo & Artisanal Clusters — Supporting cluster-based development and value-driven production
  • Food Processing & Enterprise Management — Enabling value addition, business skills, and compliance for market success

Digital Inclusion

We enable individuals and communities to access the digital ecosystem through financial literacy, digital tools, and online platforms—empowering them to connect, grow, and succeed in a rapidly evolving world.
